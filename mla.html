About Our School


MLA International school is one of the well-known education service provider in Yangon , helping Pre School to IGCSE students. It was founded in 1999 by MD and is one of the most famous schools in yangon. It is located in 127, Hanthawaddy Rd., Ward (7), Kamayut, Yangon , Myanmar. The school teaches multiple different languages and subjects helping students from pre school to IGCSE. The teachers and staff are very kind and the school is like a second home to the students.
